你的优点和缺点是什么？

My strength(斯准) is that I like to keep learning. I not only study what I learn in class, but I also plan my time to learn new things every day. I always want to get more practical skills, not just theory. Sometimes I put pressure on myself, but I think that pressure gives me motivation. My weakness is that I don’t have much hands-on experience yet — most of my knowledge comes from school. Sometimes it’s also hard to get into the learning state of mind, but I’m working to fix that. I usually go to the library five days a week, which helps me stay focused and study better.
