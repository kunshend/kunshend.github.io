为什么选择线上实习?

I chose a remote internship because it allowed me to focus more on data and AI work instead of daily office tasks. The research team provided real industrial datasets online, and I could run Python programs and deep learning models on my own system. This gave me a lot of time to practice data analysis, signal processing, and model comparison, which are very important skills for Data Analytics and AI. It also improved my self-management and communication skills, because I needed to report progress weekly and solve problems independently. I think this experience fits both programs I’m applying for — it gave me technical experience for DAAI and project coordination and reporting skills for ITM.
