毕业后你的职业目标是什么？

笼统的职业目标

I think by studying Data Analytics(爱讷莱斯特) and Artificial Intelligence, I can work in a technical job, such as data analyst or AI developer. The DAAI program focuses more on data and AI, which fits my interest and background. By studying Information Technology Management, I can also choose management or decision-making roles in the future. But I prefer technical jobs, because I think they are more stable and help me build strong skills at the beginning of my career.

DAAI的职业目标

My goal in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence is to become a data analyst or AI engineer. In my internship , I used Python, FFT, and deep learning models to analyze data and predict mechanical faults. That experience made me realize how powerful AI can be in solving real problems. I think the trend now is that AI is used everywhere, like in business and industry. To keep up, I plan to keep learning machine learning, and new AI tools. I also like creating small projects, like a simple animal-raising game I built myself — it helps me practice coding and creative thinking. So I think DAAI fits me very well, because I love using data and creativity to build something useful.

itm的职业目标

My goal in Information Technology Management is to become an IT project manager or technology consultant(肯骚tant). In my Software Engineering course project, my team built an Android job-search app, and I was responsible for the notification system and dashboard. That project taught me teamwork, planning, and communication, which are very important for management. I think the trend in ITM is digital transformation — companies are using data and AI to improve decision-making. So I want to learn how to lead technical projects effectively through the ITM program.
