毕业后你的职业目标是什么？

I think by studying Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence, I can work in a technical job, such as data analyst or AI developer. The DAAI program focuses more on data and AI, which fits my interest and background. By studying Information Technology Management, I can also choose management or decision-making roles in the future. But I prefer technical jobs, because I think they are more stable and help me build strong skills at the beginning of my career.
