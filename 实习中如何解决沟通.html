实习中如何解决沟通问题

During my internship at the Artificial Intelligence Institute, I deeply appreciated the importance of communication in research teamwork. I keep contact with WeChat and email. My supervisor required me to submit weekly reports on my learning and research, followed by video discussions.

I also summarized experimental results, such as FFT, wavelet analysis, and model accuracy. When I discovered any issues with the data or model, I communicated directly with my supervisor. This experience significantly improved my teamwork and problem-solving skills.
